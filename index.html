WHO WE ARE

Silent Disco

JJ Silent Events established in 2019 we are leading small Black Owned business experts in silent discos, silent conferences and other wireless headset events. Starting at a small base in Staten Island, New York in 2019 with 200 headsets we’ve now grown to a stock of over 1000 and cater for events right across the USA working with some huge event organizations. Our journey has taken us to some amazing places and we’ve been lucky enough to work with some fantastic clients but this is just the beginning.

Our over-ear comfortably-padded silent disco headphones are adjustable in size and each headset features a personal volume control and channel switch allowing for optional toggling between different frequencies. This Offers the guests the ability to switch genres with a tap of a button is one of the most unique features of a silent disco event. The silent disco headphone sound quality is crisp with clear highs and deep lows — perfect stereo audio regardless of position in the venue (front row or restroom ) creating an immersive and fluid atmosphere for guests to freely move around the venue without sacrificing auditory bliss.
